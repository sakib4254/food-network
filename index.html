About Us

We're a small company focused on providing tools and support for people who want to take control over their nutrition. Given the saturation of information in the diet industry, we focus on more pragmatic elements of healthy eating such as planning, shopping and cooking.

My Recipe

Very customizable meal plans allow you to enter health concerns like "low-cholesterol" and "low-sodium," something that wasn't offered on other sites. The program is broken into stages one through four, to help move you through the journey of weight loss one step at a time.
